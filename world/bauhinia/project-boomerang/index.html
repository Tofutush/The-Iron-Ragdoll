Project Boomerang / 回旋镖计划

A top-secret government project to develop the time machine. Funded by Starcorp with a lot of involvement by MSS. A pretty much independent organization called the Project Boomerang Commission was created to carry out the project. Drs. Koala and Kangaroo were the lead scientists.
