World Bauhinia Flower City

Flower City · 花城

Capital and cultural center of Bauhinia

Categories: Cities, Locations

Capital of Bauhinia, near the geographical center of the country. The flower, of course, means the bauhinia flower.

History

Flower City used to be the capital of the Bauhinian Empire too. After the revolution, the republicans redecorated the emperor's palace and simply used that as the government building.

Places of Non-Interest

  • University of Flower City
  • MSS headquarters.
