Poker Spies · 扑克间谍环

A spy ring assembled during the Fantoni War.

Categories: Misc

During the Fantoni War, MSS officer Sky ran a ring of four spies gathering intel and doing deception. Sky liked representing them with the ace cards of the four suits, which is why they are later referred to as the "Poker Spies." Read more about their stories here.

The spies

  • Tsunami: Ace of Diamonds; blue chip
  • Jumbo: Ace of Hearts; red chip
  • Mulberry: Ace of Spades; green chip
  • Blackjack: Ace of Clubs; black chip

Trivia

The inspiration behind this is Operation Fortitude1 and the wider Double-Cross System. Ben Macintyre wrote a good book about it called Double Cross.
