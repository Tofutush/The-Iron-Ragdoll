Tomato had somehow became some important Bauhinian agent in Moreland. They were the sole holder of some really important information. *Really* important. And to be safe, they were the only one who knew about it. (What specifically, IDK)

It was right before they were going to meet an important contact. Only they could meet them, because only they knew the important information.

And they fell off the stairs, hit their head, and lost their memory.

They spent a couple days roaming around in Moreland wondering who they were, before the Bauhinians retrieved them. They *really* needed to find out what Tomato remembered. But feeding their soul to someone else wouldn't work, because that person would also be amnesiac.

They sent Tomato to the soul facility, where Tangerine (Entity's friend and colleague; doesn't have a profile here) extracted their soul and sent their body to cremation.

They gave the [CRS](/world/bauhinia/crs/) a deadline. Here's Tomato's soul. We *need* whatever info they know, and the traditional way of consuming won't work.

Starlight got to work, trying to figure out how to extract Tomato's memories, which will also help her in examining her mother's memories.

After hearing about how they managed to serialize a soul into data, they sent Sparrow, a computer science specialist, to join the research program. They were very cheerful and too bright on the eyes, which annoyed Starlight.
