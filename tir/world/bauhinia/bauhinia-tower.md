The Bauhinia Tower, located in the heart of Flower City, used to be just another sightseeing place for emperors of the Bauhinian Empire, accessible only to royalty and nobles.

After the revolution, it became a tourist attraction, as people marveled from the view of the topmost level and scholars studied ancient Bauhinian architecture and culture.

After Kismet became president, it became once again shut off from the general public. At the highest level, he could nearly see the edges of the city with his hyperawareness, and Kismet used both that and his mind-reading skills to listen in on the entirety of Flower City to catch dissidence. He had been spending more and more time there, especially after obtaining Tundra's body as he can give orders to the fake Kismets remotely.
