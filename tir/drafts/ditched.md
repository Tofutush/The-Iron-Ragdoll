But how can Goose do it, being an outsider, not knowing the intricate politics in Bauhinia's ancient capital? The city is like a tight-knit flower bud, dead before it could bloom, rotting from the inside, refusing to let any outsiders in. Look at that barbarian from Coastline. A place with no roots, no history, unlike Flower City, culturally rich, with beautiful ancient poems behind every wall, hot-blooded revolutionary history on every street, and epic tales of desperation and hope around every corner.
