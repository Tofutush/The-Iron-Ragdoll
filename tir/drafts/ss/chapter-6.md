I spent the past few weeks of school busily paying attention in class, paying attention to Sugarcane, and trying to stay on top of my schoolwork, which was proving harder and harder because why did *every* teacher somehow think we have no other classes and pile work on us like there was no tomorrow? The language teacher, despite her incompetence at technology, was way too competent at assigning essays and reading. The math teacher seemingly found it funny to have a quiz at the start of *every* class, which he didn't even grade himself. Sky, oh Sky was the worst. He would completely and thoroughly torture us in and out of class, and walk away *whistling*.

But Sugarcane was there to help me, like always.
