There wasn't enough space for me to set my bag down at ease, so I asked, "Sugarcane, can you open my bag and take out the flashlights?"

Except it was Sunny who answered. "Okay, let me."

No, I didn't ask *him* to do it! I asked for Sugarcane! But Sunny was already unzipping my bag and rummaging through it.

"Got them," he said as he pulled out all three and zipped my bag back up.
