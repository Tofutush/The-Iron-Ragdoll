"Welcome to your first class of middle school," Sky said. "Tradecraft. This will undoubtedly be the most important class in this school, given our nature, but I don't want you slacking in any other classes either." He scanned his gaze sternly across the room.

"Allow me to make a proper introduction. My name is Sky Elmwood Dazzle, reserve officer of the Ministry of State Security. I am the head teacher of class five, and I will be teaching both you and class four tradecraft and politics. I work full-time here at Ministry Affiliate, so you can always find me in my office on the fourth floor. I'm in the one labeled 'tradecraft team 2;' you can find my name by the door to make sure. Some other teachers are only part-time, so you might not be able to find them when you need to. If said teacher works at the MSS and it's something urgent, I might be able to send them a message."

I noticed another box near the foot of the teacher's desk. Were those our textbooks? Why didn't he give it to us yesterday?

"So, tradecraft." Sky said.
