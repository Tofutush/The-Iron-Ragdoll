"Hear this music?" Sky asked as the bell ended. "That's the preparation bell, but in my class it's the bell for class. So," he addressed the students slipping in right now, "in the future, come in class later than this bell, and you will be considered late."

Sugarcane leaned in and whispered, "what an ass."

I snickered. Sugarcane should be the last person to worry about this; she was always in class ahead of time, her textbook and notebook ready. Except this time she only had new notebook, as we didn't have any textbook.

That was when I noticed another box near the foot of the teacher's desk. Were those our textbooks? Why didn't he give it to us yesterday?
