They sat down around the dinner table. Frosting made tea.

Sky

I am here on behalf of the the Middle School for Young Officers in Training Affiliated to the Ministry of State Security.

Sky

I know it's a mouthful. We usually call it Ministry Affiliate.

Sky

Your daughter is about to graduate elementary school. We are giving her an offer to attend middle school here.

Frosting

Really?

Sky

You're not mistaken. Our administration sees potential in a student like Harmony.

Harmony stared at him dumbfoundedly.

Harmony

What's Ministry Affiliate?

Sky

It's a special school ran by the Ministry of State Security, where you can start early in your training to become an officer there.

Harmony

Um. Why do I want to go there?

Sky

Because we'll teach you things you'd never learn anywhere else. You get to participate in missions, catching spies that are damaging our national security. And finally you can graduate into becoming an officer just like me!

Harmony

But I was supposed to go to the same school as my friend.

Sky

Sugarcane? I just visited her yesterday. She agreed to attend.

Harmony caught her parents eyeing each other.

Frosting

…I can see why. Sugarcane is a bright kid.

Harmony coughed.

Frosting

Of course, you're amazing too! That's why uncle Sky came to you too!

Sky

Indeed.

Harmony

If Sugarcane is going, then I am too.

Entity

Do we have time to consider?

Sky produced a brochure from his pocket. It was an introduction of the school.

Sky

Of course. You have until the first of August.
