About

Jackpot's mischevious and ambitious son.

Snake has a mutation that gives him a pair of bird wings.

He is very full of himself, but still likes being friends with other people a lot, so he is more pleasant to be around than his dad.

He still lives with his dad because he's too young, but he dreams of leaving him, starting his own business, and living in a big house all on his own.

He has a pet snake named Blizzard. It is -- surprise -- a Blizzard Cornsnake. He thinks snakes are cool, which is why he also named himself Snake.
