About

Frosting grew up in the Project Boomerang laboratory with her parents.

She grew up to be a government archivist, tasked with erasing records of disappeared people in addition to whatever archivists usually do. She also manages the soul vault.

She let Jerboa into the archives to read about Project Boomerang and his parents.

Relationships

Tephra (acquaintance)

Tephra and Frosting met during some case, and Tephra learned that Frosting grew up in the Project Boomerang lab just like Jerboa. He arranged a meeting for them to meet.

Jerboa (acquaintance)

They grew up in the Project Boomerang labs together, though Jerboa barely remembered Frosting and Frosting only distantly remembered that Jaculus had a little brother too.

Jerboa met Frosting through Tephra because of his promise to help Jerboa find his family.

Frosting risked her job letting Jerboa look through the archives, partly because she was bored as fuck and partly because she thought that by having Jerboa find out the truth, she was giving her dear friend Jaculus a proper closure too.

Jaculus (BFF)

Jaculus and Frosting were one of the few kids in the Project Boomerang laboratory, so they were naturally close to each other.

Entity (husband)

They worked in the same building and met each other by accident. What followed was pure adolescent (they were in their 20s LOL) passion, with them both running towards each other. Their love was so intense that it overflowed into a third person, and Harmony was born.

Honey-flavored months stretched into stale, tasteless years, and their passion burnt out into embers. They had settled into a comfortable, silent routine, where the sweet words, extravagant gifts, and the feeling of your skin against mine of their youth were unnecessary. Their love had turned steady, like an old, reliable flame that no longer blazes but refuses to die.
