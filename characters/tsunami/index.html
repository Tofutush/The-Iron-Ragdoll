About

Tsunami lost her mother at age 9 and to this day she is still convinced that she went to jail. She hated her father and loved her little brother. She got a job in a hotel.

When Crackers got back from the battle field in a box, she was heartbroken. But she was more surprised when Crackers's friend from middle school, Sky, approached her to inform her of the cause of his death — magic. And an offer to help him investigate.

This is how Tsunami started her mission in Fanton, trying to find the source of the Fantoni's magic.
