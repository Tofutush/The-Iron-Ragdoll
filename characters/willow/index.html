Relationships

Sparky (older half-sister)

Sparky and Willow have the same mother but different fathers. Willow is the one who understands Sparky the most, because of her mind-reading; but she has a hard time expressing how she felt about Sparky, especially with her being so irritable.

After Amber died and before Peacock sent Sparky to Fancy Private School, Sparky had to walk Willow there every day. At first she made it extremely clear that she blamed Peacock's entire blood-related family for Amber's death, but slowly she took a liking towards Willow.

After Willow's fiasco with Sundae, Sparky shut herself into the closet.

Fantasy (older stepsister)

Fantasy and Willow have the same father but different mothers.

Ironheart (older stepbrother)

Willow and Ironheart have the same father but different mothers.

Sundae (girlfriend?)

Willow met Sundae when she was in middle school and Sundae in college. They often sneaked out to meet each other (mostly Willow, since Sundae doesn't really need to sneak).

Willow's entire family (mainly, Peacock, Sparky, Ironheart, and Fantasy) was against their relationship, either because Willow was too young, or homophobia, or both.

Eventually they split up because 15-year-olds just should be sleeping in and being a total idiot rather than dating.
