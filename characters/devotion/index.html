About

Always parading around a poker face, it's hard to believe Devotion doesn't have a heart made of lead. If she has her sights on something, no one in the world could change her mind. This was why she enlisted in the army during the Fantoni war. Hyrax went through a lot of trouble ensuring she got out alive.

Her weapon of choice is a retired military Colt 1911.
