About

A grouchy psychologist researching on how to read souls from the outset in Flower City's Center for Research on Souls (CRS).

She had always been distant from her mother and wanted to connect, but hadn't had a chance before her mother was dying of cancer. Starlight illegally extracted her soul — in the shape of a solid rock — before she died. Starlight tried consuming it, but every single time, her body had rejected her mother's soul and she spit the chunk of rock out. She had kept it ever since, desperate to find a way to read it. The research she's doing right now thus became important to her not only professionally, but also personally.
