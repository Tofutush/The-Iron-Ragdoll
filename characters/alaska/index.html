About

They're nearly always cheerful and happy-go-lucky looking. Don't be fooled by that. They will backstab you (or even front-stab you) every time they get the chance.

They're the imitation thief who stole Myriad's lipstick and Sky's poker cards in the pickpocket case. But Sparky framed Myriad for everything instead.
