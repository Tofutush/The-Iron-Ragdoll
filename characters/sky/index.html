About

Sky is an adventurous boy who cannot stop moving.

He likes cardistry and is nearly always twiddling a desk of cards in his right hand.

His preferred weapon is a set of four cards made of steel with sharp edges, which act like throwing stars. The cards are aces of each suit.

He is really into stuff he considers cool, like photography, military, and science. He ended up looking nerdy.

After an encounter with the MSS in middle school, he was given a chance to attend high school at Ministry Affiliate. He graduated with excellent performance and was recruited into the ranks of the MSS with Goose as his mentor. He then went on to create a legend during the Fantoni Invasion War, which earned him a rocket promotion from Half-Noob straight to Old Grouch (skipping Normal Rank). Afterwards, he spent the rest of his days teaching at Ministry Affiliate. During recess, he loved showing off his cardistry skills to his students, and then adamantly refusing to teach them, quoting, "I just spent 40 minutes teaching you! Now you like my teaching?"

Relationships

Sparky (friend)

Sparky got assigned to sit next to Sky on their first day of Middle School. The stuff he did — twirl cards, take photos, being able to recognize any sort of tank at one glance — fascinated her. Though she wouldn't admit it, she had a little crush on him. The crush later turned to a big crush on Nightsun.

As for Sky, he is always looking for new friends.

Qibli (childhood friend)

Qibli and Sky have known each other since kindergarten. Their parents, Donut and Dazzle, are colleagues, which is why they often put Qibli and Sky together. Embarrasingly, they used to say they wanted to marry each other when they grew up. They no longer want to do that.

They're both nerdy as fuck, but Sky thinks it's cool while Qibli thinks she is boring. Sky would try all sort of things to convince her being nerdy is cool.

Firecrackers (friend)

At first, Sky was annoyed that he had to learn sign language to understand Firecrackers, and insisted that he just doesn't have to listen to Firecrackers. But then he realized he was missing out on all the jokes Firecrackers told, and with that Sky warmed up to him.
