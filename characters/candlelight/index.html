Relationships

Pumpkin (boyfriend)

Pumpkin and Candlelight met each other on a train to their little village in Westavia. Pumpkin, being on the run from the MSS, used a fake name "Curly."

Candlelight had just suffered a break-up and the two quickly (well, the train was very long and boring) fell in love with each other.

Sundew (older sister)

Candlelight loved Sundew, attached himself to Sundew's butt, and after she was gone attached himself to Mooncake's butt. He misses her deeply, and want to go looking for her, though at the same time he was worried he would bring their family troubles back to her.

Mooncake (childhood friend)

Candlelight and Mooncake grew up together. When Candlelight came back to his village after quitting his police job, he was so shocked to find Mooncake also a cop and doing perfectly well. Mooncake was laughing so hard at him. But at the same time she missed him and was glad he came back.
