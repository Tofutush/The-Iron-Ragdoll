About

A lovely little girl who loves art and animals, especially her winghorse Muffin. Always the one to get kidnapped during incidents. Her weapon of choice is a lasso which she can only wield effectively when she's riding.

Relationships

Sparky (friend)

Sparky and Elegance are good friends. There was this time when Elegance was kidnapped, and Sparky refused to leave her side, so she got kidnapped with her.

Sparky's superstrength distorts Elegance's perception of time, though they didn't know this at first.

Chastity (older sister)

Chastity would often threaten to slice up Elegance's plushies with her chainsaw.
