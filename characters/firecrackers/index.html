About

Firecrackers likes cursing even more than Sparky does. It's better when he's directly speaking to you; he would only stick a lot of middle fingers at you. But when he's texting, you can expect a curse every sentence.

His dad runs a little grocery store, and he often manned it for them during his spare time. His mom was missing, and his dad won't tell him where she is.

He was drafted into the army during the Fantoni invasion despite his muteness, because he didn't have a disability certificate given out by a hospital that can prove it. He was killed during battle by one of the spells Qibli sold to Fanton.

Sparky (friend)

What's the difference between their cursing? Sparky does it unconsciously; she treats curses like like normal words. But Firecrackers curses intentionally, just to piss people off.

Sparky had always whether intentionally or unintentionally tried to offer Firecrackers money, be it spare change or buying a lot of stuff at his parents' store, for which they need to pay Starcorp rent. Sparky had tried and failed to convince Peacock to lower rent for Firecrackers specifically.
