About

She is a huge pussy. Overthinks everything, tries to chicken out of more, always worrying about how things could go wrong.

She has magic, an extremely rare superpower. Her wand is a cheap plastic toy bought when she was three years old, a purple five-point star on a stick.

She's also supposed to represent me by the way.

She's really nerdy. She likes spy thrillers and computer programming, though she sucks at it. She also loves reading and drawing comics.

Relationships

Sparky (friend)

Sparky and Qibli became friends through Sky. She is always trying to pull Qibli out of her own world and make her hang out with Sky and Firecrackers.

Sky (childhood friend)

Qibli and Sky have known each other since kindergarten. Their parents, Donut and Dazzle, are colleagues, which is why they often put Qibli and Sky together. Embarrasingly, they used to say they wanted to marry each other when they grew up. They no longer want to do that.

They're both nerdy as fuck, but Sky thinks it's cool while Qibli thinks she is boring. Sky would try all sort of things to convince her being nerdy is cool.

Firecrackers (friend)

Trinket (ex-friend / classmate)

Qibli and Trinket met in the Magic Academy. Trinket's magic is far more powerful than Qibli's, and she would teach Qibli to do a lot of spells she figured out on her own.

Qibli and Trinket's biggest difference is this: Trinket is mischevious and annoying to adults, but she's not a rule-breaker. Qibli is quiet and the teacher's pet, but she would break the law in a heartbeat -- it would even give her the thrills.

That's how come their friendship broke over the discovery of A Guide to Magic. Qibli loved it. Trinket wouldn't let her touch it.

After Qibli murdered Nebula and made away with the book, Trinket, barely 18, was made the new Arch-Magician. She vowed to get Qibli if it's the last thing she does.

Nebula (teacher)

Nebula had thought Qibli would be an easy kid. She was wrong.

Qibli never liked Nebula's fakey cheerfulness. When she found A Guide to Magic and decided to run away with it, she found out how Nebula cast an irreversible charm on it to inform her of its location. She was left with no choice but to murder Nebula.
