Relationships

Goose (subordinate)

Halcyon and Goose are on the same team. Goose trusted and looked up to Halcyon, treating him like another shifu; and Halcyon kept her in the dark about his treason.

When Halcyon was outed as a traitor and a murderer, Goose felt betrayed. She poured all her anger into the interrogation, torturing him into spitting out his soul, until he spilled all his secrets.

If Halcyon's betrayal had merely been "business" mixed with a bit of bribery and coercion before, now he had nothing but animosity towards Bauhinia and its system. As for Goose, this was just the start of her journey of becoming the queen of torture.
