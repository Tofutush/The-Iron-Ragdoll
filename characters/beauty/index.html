About

The oldest daughter in the Cherry-Hyrax family, always neck-deep in pop culture. Her desk is plastered with pictures of her favorite idols, and she would never miss a single concert, unless she got bad grades at school and Hyrax forbid her from going.

She has strabismus, where her right eye points outward. Her weapon of choice is a bladed fan.

Hyrax and Peacock arranged for her to marry Ironheart.
