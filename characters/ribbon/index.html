About

Ribbon is a cute girl who loves baking and taught Sparky to cook. Though she isn't taking care of her for nothing!

She has a huge crush on Starry and is determined to not only find out who attacked him, but also make the assiliant regret what they did for the rest of their life.

Also she's a murderer herself.

Relationships

Sparky

Ribbon and Sparky have the same birthday, though Ribbon was two years older than Sparky. Ribbon showed Sparky the ropes around Hillslope, taught her to cook and bake, and asked her to investigate the attack on Starry. Sparky was about to take all the caring she gave her for granted. She couldn't have been more mistaken.

Gratitude gets you far, Sparky. Very far.

Starry (crush)

Ribbon has a huge crush on Starry. (Before he got knocked out, or it's gonna be creepy.) After he went into the coma, Ribbon took his room key and took care of him, keeping him alive every day by jamming water and soup down his throat with a funnel. If Starry wasn't aware of her crush before, he certainly is now.
