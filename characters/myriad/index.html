About

Myriad has a mutation that gives her a pair of fairy wings. She's really insecure about it, worried that people would only try to use her for that, so she hides them tucked inside her backpack which she never takes off.

She's also a kleptomaniac. She holds herself back from stealing actually valuable stuff, but she loves pocketing pens and rulers and stuff. She also stole a lot of her parents' stuff, like a lipstick tube from her mom, which in turn got stolen by the imitator.

Relationships

Sparky (classmates)

Myriad worked with Sparky on the thief case. They had a fair share of conflicts, as Myriad disapproved of Sparky's recklessness and Sparky thought Myriad was dull. In the end, Sparky framed Myriad for all the theft and got her sent to juvie.
