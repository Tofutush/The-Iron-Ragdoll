About

She cries pearls. Yes, her tears are literally pearls.

She is everything you would expect a little girl to be: cute, curious, instinctively kind, loves animals, and incredibly dumb.

Relationships

Sparky

Sparky took care of Pearl like she's her daughter. However, as they ran out of money and was struggling to move forward in Sylvandia, she found herself also looking at Pearl and seeing not a person, but a machine for producing pearls.

Snake (friend)

Pearl and Snake quickly became friends, because for both of them, it was like finally seeing a kid their own age after a life of isolation.
