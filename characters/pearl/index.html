About

She cries pearls. Yes, her tears are literally pearls.

She is everything you would expect a little girl to be: cute, curious, instinctively kind, loves animals, and incredibly dumb.

Biography

Pearl was kidnapped from her hometown in Snowdale, Sylvandia, after Starcorp discovered that she cried pearl tears. She was kept in a basement and tortured every day to produce pearls. Fantasy's necklace was made from her tears.

After Sparky discovered this, she tried to send her home, but failed and they were recaptured.

Stories

Pearl's Journey Home

Canon

The friends send a kidnapped girl home, all the way across the continent. When the journey ends, everything stays the same, and yet no one is the same anymore.

Relationships

Sparky

Sparky took care of Pearl like she's her daughter. However, as they ran out of money and was struggling to move forward in Sylvandia, she found herself also looking at Pearl and seeing not a person, but a machine for producing pearls.

Snake (friend)

Pearl and Snake quickly became friends, because for both of them, it was like finally seeing a kid their own age after a life of isolation.
