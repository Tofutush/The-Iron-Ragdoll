About

Jackalope is an officer at the Intelligence Corps of the Guardian Moose. She is classified as an extrovert.

In her free time, she loved gardening.

Relationships

Goose (arch nemesis)

Goose and Jackalope had never met each other. They only know each other by code names. If they were to meet, they would have become great friends; but they became enemies because of the countries they respectively served.

Maybe on late, sleepless nights, they would lie awake wondering what the girl over there looked like. Did she like gardening too? Did she cry when the protagonists of that trashy soap opera broke up too? What kind of a person was she?

They could look into the iron curtain and stare at their reflections. That might as well be her.

Jinx (girlfriend / comrade)

Despite being in different units (Jackalope the infantry and Jinx the cavalry), they became friends. They planned to stay with the Guardians together, but Jinx didn't pass and had to leave. Jacklope stayed in contact with her.

Jacklape has to admit that she was the one who convinced the Guardians with some fake "bad luck" bullshit to not let Jinx stay, out of selfish reasons — if Jinx stayed, they would not be allowed to date each other.
