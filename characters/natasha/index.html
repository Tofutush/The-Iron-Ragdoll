About

Natasha is loud. You'll hear her before you see her. And after you see her, you'll probably feel her. Or feel whatever thing she managed to throw at you.

She seems addicted to attention. She couldn't handle not being in the spotlight. She probably doesn't realize she's not the only person in the world. But she does; she knows that all too clearly. She's like this for a reason. She barely sees her parents, as they're both working in the city. Her grandparents weren't the most fun people in the world by young people standards, and she didn't have any siblings. She was lonely, and she craved attention.
