About

Goose is just like other girls. She likes everything other girls like. She does everything other girls do. She listens to whatever music is most popular at the moment. She watches whatever show is most popular at the moment.

Goose is just happy to spend her life on the bandwagon.

Goose is an officer at the Ministry of State Security. She likes juggling her baton and giving vague threats about sending people to a possible Ministry of Love. She has a knack of trying her best at everything she puts her mind to do.

She 's 29 but still lives with her parents. If they died she would bury herself with them.

Relationships

Sky (apprentice)

Goose's first very own apprentice is Sky.

Pumpkin (colleague / friend)

Pumpkin and Goose are on the same team in the MSS, both on the younger side. They have a very pure and goofy friendship, always willing to help each other out, and always ready to laugh at each other when they do something dumb (which is often).

Pumpkin's hyperawareness also blocks Goose's invisibility, so Goose would often hide herself and wait for Pumpkin to poke her into tangibility and they would giggle and Paperweight would yell at them.

Paperweight (shifu)

Paperweight is constantly annoyed that Goose didn't live up to his expectations.

Trivia

  • She gives off a goofy villain vibe.
  • She loves juggling her baton, kicking it from one foot to the other, catching it on her chin, spinning it on her fingertips, and throwing it at random colleagues (usually Pumpkin).
