About

Check out her PMV!

Goose is just like other girls. She likes everything other girls like. She does everything other girls do. She listens to whatever music is most popular at the moment. She watches whatever show is most popular at the moment.

Goose is just happy to spend her life on the bandwagon.

Goose is an officer at the Ministry of State Security. She likes juggling her baton and giving vague threats about sending people to a possible Ministry of Love. She has a knack of trying her best at everything she puts her mind to do. She never fails to take business personally.

She 's 29 but still lives with her parents. If they died she would bury herself with them.

Relationships

Sky (apprentice)

Goose's first very own apprentice is Sky.

Pumpkin (colleague / friend)

Pumpkin and Goose are on the same team in the MSS, both on the younger side. They have a very pure and goofy friendship, always willing to help each other out, and always ready to laugh at each other when they do something stupid (which is often).

When Goose found out about Pumpkin's treason after the Moreland nuke fiasco, she took it too personally — why didn't Pumpkin tell me about it when Halcyon first demanded him to steal stuff from Paperweight? Why did he keep it to himself? The betrayal pained Goose.

Halcyon (superior)

Halcyon and Goose are on the same team. Goose trusted and looked up to Halcyon, treating him like another mentor; and Halcyon kept her in the dark about his treason.

When Halcyon was outed as a traitor and a murderer, Goose felt betrayed. She poured all her anger into the interrogation, torturing him into spitting out his soul, until he spilled all his secrets.

If Halcyon's betrayal had merely been "business" mixed with a bit of bribery and coercion before, now he had nothing but animosity towards Bauhinia and its system. As for Goose, this was just the start of her journey of becoming the queen of torture.

Paperweight (mentor)

Paperweight is constantly annoyed that Goose didn't live up to his expectations, yet he uses her as free labor all the time.

Jackalope (arch nemesis)

Goose and Jackalope had never met each other. They only know each other by code names. If they were to meet, they would have become great friends; but they became enemies because of the countries they respectively served.

Maybe on late, sleepless nights, they would lie awake wondering what the girl over there looked like. Did she like gardening too? Did she cry when the protagonists of that trashy soap opera broke up too? What kind of a person was she?

They could look into the iron curtain and stare at their reflections. That might as well be her.

  • If she had a lusus, it would be a goose.
