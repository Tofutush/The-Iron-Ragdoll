About

The dictator president of Bauhinia. Extremely powerful.

Kismet was born with nothing special, but he consumed 5 other souls with extremely strong superpowers and now he has all 5 common powers. He is after Tundra's immortal body. If magic weren't impossible to get ahold of, he would've went for that too.

His 6 souls made him into a rainbow and molded his text color into pure black.

He has his extremely powerful mind-reading tuned to the entire Flower City. Usually people would just be background noise, but he would be alerted if there are insubservient thoughts.

He will also order the eternal death of anyone who learns his name, outside of a select whitelist.
