About

Sundew was the daughter of Muscat and Ember. She hated her dad and made it her life's mission to help her mom escape, but she failed.

She studied hard and got into a university in People's Vanguard, though Ember forbid her from going. She counted up her life's savings, borrowed a lot from Thyme, and stole a little bit of Ember's, and hopped on a midnight train from Area 29 to People's Vanguard. She never looked back.

Now, she owns a little mama and papa's restaurant with her husband in People's Vanguard. Life was hard but relatively peaceful. She missed her little brother and wondered how he was doing, and will he ever come looking for her.
