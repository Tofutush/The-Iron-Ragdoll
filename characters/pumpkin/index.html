About

Pumpkin is a goofy guy who loves slurping boba tea and hates filling out forms.

Relationships

Goose (colleague / friend)

Pumpkin and Goose are on the same team in the MSS, both on the younger side. They have a very pure and goofy friendship, always willing to help each other out, and always ready to laugh at each other when they do something stupid (which is often).

When Goose found out about Pumpkin's treason after the Moreland nuke fiasco, she took it too personally — why didn't Pumpkin tell me about it when Halcyon first demanded him to steal stuff from Paperweight? Why did he keep it to himself? The betrayal pained Goose.

Halcyon (mentor)

Pumpkin never thought he could've been given a job at the MSS, yet here he is. He is extremely grateful to Halcyon, respects him, and wishes to never let him down. This is why, when Halcyon started asking him to steal stuff from Paperweight and eventually more important people, he didn't decline. Pumpkin kept thinking that if he had enough spine back then, things would've turned out different. He now sees Halcyon as he is: a cowardly traitor. But who's to say Pumpkin isn't the same?

Paperweight (superior)

Paperweight hates Pumpkin because he's so annoying. Also because he's jealous at how Pumpkin was smarter and stronger than him. And that he's mad at himself for growing old.

If Pumpkin pulled out a boba tea in front of him, he would take it and dump the entire thing down the drain while forcing Pumpkin to watch.

Also he thinks Pumpkin is a weirdo because Paperweight is homophobic as fuck.

Pumpkin quite reveres Paperweight, but at the same time he was also scared of him, because fuck knows how he's going to be tortured if Paperweight finds out about his treason.

Candlelight (boyfriend)

Pumpkin and Candlelight met each other on a train to their little village in Westavia. Pumpkin, being on the run from the MSS, used a fake name "Curly."

Candlelight had just suffered a break-up and the two quickly (well, the train was very long and boring) fell in love with each other.

Helsinki (distant relative)

Helsinki's and Pumpkin's grandparents were related somewhat.
