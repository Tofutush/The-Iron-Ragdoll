Relationships

Goose (colleague / friend)

Pumpkin and Goose are on the same team in the MSS, both on the younger side. They have a very pure and goofy friendship, always willing to help each other out, and always ready to laugh at each other when they do something dumb (which is often).

Pumpkin's hyperawareness also blocks Goose's invisibility, so Goose would often hide herself and wait for Pumpkin to poke her into tangibility and they would giggle and Paperweight would yell at them.

Halcyon (shifu)

Paperweight (superior)

Paperweight hates Pumpkin because he's so annoying. Also because he's jealous at how Pumpkin was smarter and stronger than him. And that he's mad at himself for growing old.

If Pumpkin pulled out a boba tea in front of him, he would take it and dump the entire thing down the drain while forcing Pumpkin to watch.

Also he thinks Pumpkin is a weirdo because Paperweight is homophobic as snabbergatt.

Pumpkin quite reveres Paperweight, and is especially curious about the "legend" he created. At the same time he was also scared of him, because snabbergatt knows how he's going to be tortured if Paperweight finds out about his treason.

Candlelight (boyfriend)

Pumpkin and Candlelight met each other on a train to their little village in Westavia. Pumpkin, being on the run from the MSS, used a fake name "Curly."

Candlelight had just suffered a break-up and the two quickly (well, the train was very long and boring) fell in love with each other.

Helsinki (distant relative)

Helsinki's and Pumpkin's grandparents were related somewhat.
