About

Vampire was one of the few people to jump over the dragon gate, situated in Atlasia, before Bauhinian magicians stole borrowed it. He was 13 when he turned into a dragon this way. He somehow unknowingly made it to Coastline through a system of underwater rivers, and have been depleting the fish of Honeypot Lake for 15 years before he was found.
