About

Tundra found out early on in his life that he was immortal. Because of this, he didn't take care of his body at all; not even lending worry to his fatal thing. He spent his days drinking and smoking weed in corners the law can't see.

None of this prepared him for when the dictator of Bauhinia himself went after him.
