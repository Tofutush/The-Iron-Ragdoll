About

Jerboa grew up in the Project Boomerang labs with his family. Later, his parents disappeared from the facility, and he was sent to Coastline and adopted by Jam.

He had taken up many professions, such as being a bartender, chemist, and undertaker. He really wants to find out what happened to his family. His most prized possession is a family photo of his parents, his sister, and him.

boss

Tephra promised to help Jerboa find his parents if Jerboa was willing to stick around his butt and do his bidding all day. Years later, Tephra became a cop, and fulfilled his promise by introducing Jerboa to Frosting.

Frosting

acquaintance

They grew up in the Project Boomerang labs together, though Jerboa barely remembered Frosting and Frosting only distantly remembered that Jaculus had a little brother too.

Frosting risked her job letting Jerboa look through the archives, partly because she was bored as fuck and partly because she thought that by having Jerboa find out the truth, she was giving her dear friend Jaculus a proper closure too.
