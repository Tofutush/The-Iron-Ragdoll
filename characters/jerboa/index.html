About

Jerboa grew up in the Project Boomerang labs with his family. Later, his parents disappeared from the facility, and he was sent to Coastline and adopted by Jam.

Relationships

Tephra (boss)

Tephra promised to help Jerboa find his parents if Jerboa was willing to stick around his butt and do his bidding all day. Years later, Tephra became a cop, and fulfilled his promise by introducing Jerboa to Frosting.

Jam (adoptive father)

Jam adopted Jerboa after his parents and older sister were murdered.

Koala (mother)

Jerboa doesn't have memories of his mom. His only proof of her existence is a family photo he doesn't remember taking.

Kangaroo (father)

Jerboa doesn't have memories of his dad. His only proof of his existence is a family photo he doesn't remember taking.

Frosting (acquaintance)

They grew up in the Project Boomerang labs together, though Jerboa barely remembered Frosting and Frosting only distantly remembered that Jaculus had a little brother too.

Jerboa met Frosting through Tephra because of his promise to help Jerboa find his family.

Frosting risked her job letting Jerboa look through the archives, partly because she was bored as fuck and partly because she thought that by having Jerboa find out the truth, she was giving her dear friend Jaculus a proper closure too.

Jaculus (older sister)

Jerboa faintly remembers playing dolls with Jaculus. She is also in the family photo he doesn't remember taking.

  • Design clarifications
    • The marks on the girls' faces are eyelashes, not face paint! You can draw whatever you want; I'm just making sure you don't think it's face paint.
    • None of my characters have real-world skin colors! Their races are just whether they have ears or horns on their heads, so everyone's skin color is the same.
      • You can draw whatever color you want as long as it's either normal human color, or grayscale. My default is Chinese though for your reference.

    • Their ears are furry but not fluffy. Shorthair, if you may.
