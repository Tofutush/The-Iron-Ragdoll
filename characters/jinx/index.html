About

Jinx's mother died giving birth. Her father named her "Jinx" because of this.

Jinx made the most athiest of Moose question their beliefs — wherever she went, trouble followed.

After graduating high school, she git drafted into the cavalry for her love of horses, both normal ones and winghorses. After completing her service, she continued to work as a horse trainer and riding instructor.

Relationships

Jackalope (girlfriend / comrade)

Despite being in different units (Jackalope the infantry and Jinx the cavalry), they became friends. They planned to stay with the Guardians together, but Jinx didn't pass and had to leave. Jacklope stayed in contact with her.

Jacklape has to admit that she was the one who convinced the Guardians with some fake "bad luck" bullshit to not let Jinx stay, out of selfish reasons — if Jinx stayed, they would not be allowed to date each other.

Typhoon (instructor)

Jinx is batshit scared of Typhoon in a funny way.

Drawing Permissions

(Nearly identical to my Artfight permissions anyways.)

  • Design clarifications
    • The marks on the girls' faces are eyelashes, not face paint! You can draw whatever you want; I'm just making sure you don't think it's face paint.
    • None of my characters have real-world skin colors! Their races are just whether they have ears or horns on their heads, so everyone's skin color is the same.
      • You can draw whatever color you want as long as it's either normal human color, or grayscale. My default is Chinese though for your reference.
    • The highlights on their hair are parts of the design, and are optional.
    • Their ears are furry but not fluffy. Shorthair, if you may.
