About

Jinx's mother died giving birth. Her father named her "Jinx" because of this.

Jinx made the most athiest of Moose question their beliefs — wherever she went, trouble followed.

After graduating high school, she git drafted into the cavalry for her love of horses, both normal ones and winghorses. After completing her service, she continued to work as a horse trainer and riding instructor.
