About

Peacock is Hyrax's old friend and personal attorney.

He is also Sparky's reincarnation.

{% image 'gallery/' 'peacock exploding butt.png' 200 "Willow exploding Peacock's butt" 'right' %}

He is practical and rational, and often appears quite emotionless. Around his kids, he constantly looks like he's about to kill himself.

His job, as he described it, is "professional butt-wiper." He covers up Starcorp's illegal activities, either by framing them as legal, or making sure no one has enough evidence to implicate them. Big things don't pop up that often though, so most of his time is spent union-busting and shutting people up about general employee mistreatment.
