About

Runs the drug lab in Hillslope. He's the boss of the press, and most people of Hillslope.

But here's the thing: he's a snabbergatting dumb coward.

He saw Starry's unmoving body lying on the ground (after Alaska and immediately freaked out, thinking that Ribbon was going to accuse him. (She did.) So he ran a huge cover-up campaign which ironically implicated him.
