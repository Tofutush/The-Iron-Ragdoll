Relationships

Jerboa

Tephra promised to help Jerboa find his parents if Jerboa was willing to stick around his butt and do his bidding all day. Years later, Tephra became a cop, and fulfilled his promise by introducing Jerboa to Frosting.

Frosting (acquaintance)

Tephra and Frosting met during some case, and Tephra learned that Frosting grew up in the Project Boomerang lab just like Jerboa. He arranged a meeting for them to meet.

Melody (friend)

Ahh, I made a classic cop / detective dynamic. Melody often helps Tephra solve cases; and sometimes Tephra uses his police authority helps Melody on some of his cases too.
