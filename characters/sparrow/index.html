About

After people at the CRS found out that souls could be serialized into data, Sparrow was hired as a computer science specialist to work on the project alongside Starlight.

They're quite carefree in their demeanors, boldened by their family's connections in the government, and treat every day like "any work for me to do? No? Well, time to go play!" But when they find an interesting lead worth pursuing, they will work day and night until they're satisfied.

At first, they don't even believe in the concept of souls, thinking that someone is playing a deliberate prank on them. But later they came to accept that souls are actually a thing that exists.
