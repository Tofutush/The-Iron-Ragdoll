About

Jumbo was just a small trader living near the Fantoni border, trading stuff between Bauhinia and Fanton, mostly staying within the bounds of the law.

She loved making OCs. She created characters inside the real world, and took pride in the detailed research she does in the real world that made her OCs more realistic.

When the war broke out, trade between the two nations were nearly all cut off, and Jumbo found herself without any source of income. But the connections she established with Fantoni traders weren't going anywhere. She tried to offer to work for the MSS, but got rejected. And then she suddenly got a tiny bit of actual Bauhinian intel (details TBD), so she tried her luck again with the Fantoni, using that intel as a buy-in. They recruited her.

Jumbo then realized that she had no way of actually gathering useful intel, so she simply made shit up. When inconsistencies were found out by the National Security Agency of Fanton, she made up fake contacts based on her OCs to shift the blame to.

Slowly, due to shit tradecraft, her messages to the Fanton military got intercepted by the MSS. They had a good laugh about how stupid this was, and realized that the Fantoni Secret Service trusted Jumbo a lot. Here comes the chance. Sky went to Jumbo and gave her a choice. She can either go to jail for treason, or she can start working for the MSS, giving the Fantoni Secret Service some specific fake intel provided to her. The choice was obvious. Jumbo finally got to work for the MSS now.

After the war was over, Jumbo was too burnt out to ever consider making OCs again. She moved to northeastern Bauhinia to do trade with Atlasia.
